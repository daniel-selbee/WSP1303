Competitive Analysis

  1. When looking to gain market share, it is wise to analyze the competition and embrace (or learn from) their experience. Are they drawing in a demographic you would like to tap into? Are visitors spending more time in their site's pages than yours? Are they using keywords that you should be target as well? And, of course, any of the bad practices that are holding them back are great lessons learned for you.
